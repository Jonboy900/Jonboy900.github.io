Computer Games Industry.

Opperation and management of a small games compnay.

History

Concept and Set-up - Having worked for some very large companies a pair of us decided we could do a little better and set up Gameworld Seven Ltd to be a facilities company servicing the needs of the games industry.

Location - Baseing the company in Pembrokshire West Wales we set out to provided an online remote service.

Services - Offering a wide range of service with the ability to adapt to our clients needs from Pitch development, Asset creation, Level Design and Testing.

Clients - We developed a wide client base including Electronic Arts, Microsft, Sony. Nintendo Codemasters, Rebellion and Blitz.

Products - A few of the titles we worked on included Progect Gotham Racing, Miro Maniacs, Lego Rock Raiders, Chicken Run, The Mummy.
